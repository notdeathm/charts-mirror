# Global AeroCharts CDN

This repo contains PDF aeronautical data, Taken from the official AIP website

---

## Data Categories

### 1. Aerodrome Data (AD)
* **Contents** Runway dimensions, Communication Frequencies, Ground Services
* ``AD0.6.pdf`` Shows you the charts you need and what there name

### 2. Charts
* **Contents** SID (Departures), STAR (Arrivals), and IAC (Approch Plates).
* **Format** Organized by ICAO sub-folders (e.g /MA/GMNN/)

### 3. General Info
* **Contents** National regulations and emergency proceedures
